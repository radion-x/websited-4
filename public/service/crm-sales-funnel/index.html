Our Implementation Process

We follow a proven methodology that gets your automation live quickly while ensuring long-term success.

1

Discovery & Strategy (Week 1)

We audit your current processes, map your customer journey, and identify automation opportunities. We'll document your sales process, lead sources, pain points, and goals to create a custom automation strategy.

2

CRM Setup & Configuration (Week 2)

We set up your CRM with custom pipelines, fields, tags, and user permissions. Data migration from existing systems is handled carefully to preserve your historical information and relationships.

3

Automation Building (Week 2-3)

We build your email sequences, SMS campaigns, workflow automations, and funnel pages. Every automation is designed to move leads through your pipeline efficiently while maintaining a personal touch.

4

Integration & Testing (Week 3-4)

We connect all your tools and test every workflow exhaustively. QA testing ensures emails render correctly, triggers fire properly, and data flows seamlessly between systems.

5

Training & Launch (Week 4)

We train your team on the new system with recorded tutorials and live sessions. Launch happens with our team monitoring closely to catch and resolve any issues immediately.

6

Optimisation & Support (Ongoing)

We monitor performance, analyze results, and continuously optimise your automations. Monthly reporting shows what's working and opportunities for improvement. Ongoing support ensures your system evolves with your business.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right CRM for my business?

The right CRM depends on your business size, sales process complexity, budget, and technical capabilities. During our discovery call, we'll assess your needs and recommend the best platform. For most small-medium businesses, we recommend GoHighLevel for its all-in-one capabilities and value. Larger enterprises with complex sales processes often benefit from HubSpot or Salesforce.

Can you migrate my data from my current CRM?

Yes, we handle complete data migrations including contacts, deals, notes, activities, and historical data. We clean and deduplicate your data during migration to ensure you start fresh. We've migrated data from virtually every CRM including spreadsheets, Mailchimp, Constant Contact, and custom databases.

Will my team need extensive training?

We design systems that are intuitive to use, but yes, training is included in every implementation. We provide live training sessions, recorded video tutorials, and written documentation. Most teams are comfortable with the system within 1-2 weeks. We also offer ongoing support for questions that arise after launch.

How customizable are the automation workflows?

Highly customizable. Every workflow is built from scratch to match your specific sales process, not generic templates. We can create conditional logic based on any data point, trigger actions based on behavior, and integrate with virtually any tool. If you can describe the workflow, we can build it.

What if I already have a CRM but it's not working well?

Many businesses have CRMs that aren't properly configured or utilized. We can audit your existing system, identify issues, and optimise it to work properly. Sometimes this is more cost-effective than switching platforms. We'll give you an honest assessment of whether to optimise or migrate.

How long until I see results from automation?

You'll see immediate impact from basic automations like appointment reminders and follow-ups within the first week of launch. Lead nurture sequences typically show results within 30-60 days as leads progress through your funnel. Full ROI realization usually occurs within 90 days for most businesses.

Do you provide ongoing management or just setup?

Both options are available. We offer one-time setup packages where we build your system and train your team to manage it. We also offer ongoing management packages where we monitor performance, make optimizations, create new campaigns, and provide unlimited support. Most clients start with setup and upgrade to ongoing management as they scale.

What's included in the monthly CRM platform costs?

Platform costs vary by provider and plan. GoHighLevel starts at $97/month (or less through us as an agency). HubSpot has a free tier with paid plans from $45/month. Salesforce starts at $25/user/month. We'll help you choose the right plan for your needs and ensure you're not overpaying for features you don't need.

Can automation feel too impersonal to my customers?

Only if done poorly. We design automations that feel personal and timely, not robotic and spammy. By using personalization tokens, behavior-based triggers, and well-crafted copy, automated messages feel like they're coming from a human. The goal is to deliver the right message at the right time‚Äîsomething humans often fail to do consistently.

How much does CRM automation implementation cost?

Implementation costs vary based on complexity, platform, and scope. Basic setups start around $2,500. Comprehensive implementations with multiple funnels, complex automations, and integrations typically range from $5,000-$15,000. Ongoing management ranges from $500-$2,000/month. We'll provide a detailed quote after understanding your specific needs during the discovery call.
