Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to work?

SEO is a long-term strategy. Most clients see initial improvements within 3 months, significant results by 6 months, and strong ROI by 12 months. Competitive industries may take longer. We focus on sustainable growth rather than risky shortcuts that could result in penalties.

Do you guarantee first page rankings?

No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings‚ÄîGoogle's algorithm considers over 200 factors we don't control. What we guarantee is following best practices, transparent reporting, and continuous optimisation. Our track record speaks for itself: most clients achieve page 1 rankings for their target keywords.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO generates organic (free) traffic by improving your rankings in natural search results‚Äîit takes time but provides long-term value. PPC (Google Ads) provides immediate visibility through paid ads‚Äîyou pay for each click. Most businesses benefit from both: PPC for immediate leads while SEO builds over time.

What's Local SEO vs regular SEO?

Local SEO specifically targets customers in your geographic area and focuses on the Google Map Pack, Google Business Profile, and location-based searches. Regular SEO targets broader keywords regardless of location. Service businesses, retail stores, and restaurants need Local SEO; online-only businesses focus on regular SEO.

How do you choose which keywords to target?

We analyze search volume, competition, relevance to your business, and commercial intent. We prioritize keywords that will drive revenue, not just traffic. We also consider your current rankings, competitor gaps, and the realistic timeline to rank. You'll approve all target keywords before we begin.

Do I need to keep doing SEO forever?

SEO requires ongoing effort to maintain results. Competitors are always optimising, Google's algorithm constantly evolves, and content needs refreshing. However, once you've built authority, the maintenance effort is less intensive than the initial push. Many clients scale down to maintenance mode after achieving their goals.

How do you build backlinks?

We use white-hat methods only: digital PR, guest posting on quality sites, creating link-worthy content, industry directories, and relationship-based outreach. We never buy links from PBNs or engage in link schemes that could trigger Google penalties. Quality over quantity, always.

How do you measure SEO success?

We track multiple metrics: keyword rankings, organic traffic, conversion rates, leads generated, and revenue attributed to SEO. Rankings matter, but ultimately we measure success by business outcomes‚Äîare you getting more customers and revenue from organic search?

What industries do you have SEO experience in?

We've worked across nearly every industry including professional services, healthcare, home services, e-commerce, real estate, hospitality, and B2B. SEO principles apply universally, but we tailor strategies to industry-specific search behavior and competition. Ask about case studies in your industry.

How much does SEO cost?

SEO investment varies based on competition, goals, and scope. Our packages typically start from $1,500/month for local businesses and $3,000/month for competitive industries. We'll provide a custom quote based on your specific situation. Consider it an investment with compounding returns over time.
