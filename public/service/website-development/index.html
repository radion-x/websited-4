Our Website Development Process

A proven 6-phase process that delivers on time, on budget, and beyond expectations. We keep you involved at every stage.

1

Discovery & Strategy (Week 1)

We dive deep into your business, goals, target audience, and competition. We'll define your unique value proposition, key conversion actions, and content requirements. You'll receive a detailed project plan with timeline and milestones.

2

Design & Wireframes (Week 2-3)

We create wireframes showing page structure and user flow, then develop full visual designs. You'll see exactly how your site will look on desktop, tablet, and mobile before we write any code. Unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.

3

Development (Week 3-5)

Our developers bring your designs to life with clean, optimised code. We build all functionality, integrate your tools, and optimise for speed. You'll have access to a staging site to review progress throughout development.

4

Content & SEO (Week 4-5)

We implement your content (or write it for you with our copywriting add-on), optimise all pages for your target keywords, set up analytics tracking, and ensure all technical SEO elements are in place.

5

Testing & Launch (Week 5-6)

Rigorous testing across browsers, devices, and screen sizes. We check all forms, links, integrations, and page speeds. Once you approve, we handle the launchâ€”DNS, SSL, redirects, and all technical details.

6

Training & Support (Ongoing)

We train your team on managing content and provide documentation. After launch, we monitor performance, fix any issues, and provide 30 days of complimentary support. Ongoing maintenance packages available.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed in 4-6 weeks from project kickoff. Simple landing pages can be ready in 2 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom builds may take 8-12 weeks. Timing also depends on how quickly you provide content and feedback. We'll give you a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do I own my website when it's done?

Yes, absolutely. You own 100% of your websiteâ€”the design, code, and content are yours. We don't hold your site hostage with proprietary systems. You'll have full admin access and can host it anywhere (though we offer competitive hosting if you prefer). You're never locked in.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Yes. Every website we build includes a user-friendly content management system (CMS). We'll train you on how to update text, add images, create blog posts, and manage basic content. For more complex changes, our support team is always available to help.

What do I need to provide to get started?

At minimum, we need your logo, brand guidelines (if any), examples of websites you like, and information about your services. Content (text and images) can come laterâ€”we'll guide you through the process. We also offer copywriting and photography services if you need them.

Do you provide hosting? What does it cost?

We offer managed hosting starting from $30/month for standard sites and $60/month for e-commerce. This includes fast Australian servers, SSL, daily backups, security monitoring, and WordPress/plugin updates. You're welcome to use your own hosting provider if preferredâ€”we'll help with setup.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Every website we build is mobile-first, meaning we design for mobile devices first, then scale up to tablets and desktops. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, this approach is essential for both user experience and Google rankings.

Is SEO included with my website?

Every website includes on-page SEO foundations: optimised titles, meta descriptions, schema markup, fast loading, mobile-friendliness, and proper site structure. For ongoing SEO services (content creation, link building, rank tracking), check out our SEO & Local SEO service.

What if I need changes after the website launches?

You get 30 days of complimentary support after launch for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, you can use pay-as-you-go support ($120/hour) or sign up for a monthly maintenance plan that includes updates, security, and support hours. Most clients choose the maintenance plan for peace of mind.

Can you integrate my website with other tools?

Yes. We regularly integrate with CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel), email platforms (Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign), booking systems (Calendly, Acuity), payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), accounting software (Xero, MYOB), and more. If there's an API, we can usually connect it.

What's the payment structure?

Standard terms are 50% deposit to begin, 50% on launch. For larger projects, we offer milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfer, credit card, and can arrange payment plans for qualified businesses. The deposit secures your spot in our development schedule.
