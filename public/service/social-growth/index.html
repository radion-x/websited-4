Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between organic social and paid social?

Organic social refers to free content posted on your profiles - building community, brand awareness, and engagement over time. Paid social is advertising - paying to reach specific audiences beyond your followers. Most businesses need both: organic builds long-term brand equity while paid drives immediate results and scales reach.

How much should I budget for social media advertising?

Budget depends on your goals, industry, and competition. For testing, we recommend starting with $2,000-5,000/month in ad spend across platforms. This allows enough data for optimisation. As campaigns prove ROI, we scale budgets accordingly. We'll provide specific recommendations based on your goals during the strategy phase.

Which platforms should my business be on?

It depends on where your audience spends time and your business type. B2B businesses typically focus on LinkedIn and Google. E-commerce thrives on Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. Local services benefit from Facebook and Google. We'll recommend the right mix based on your audience research and goals.

How long before I see results from social media marketing?

Paid campaigns can generate results within days - traffic, leads, and sales. However, optimisation takes 2-4 weeks as we gather data. Organic growth is slower - expect 3-6 months to build meaningful engagement and follower growth. We set realistic timelines based on your specific goals and starting point.

Do you create the content or do we provide it?

We offer both options. Our team can handle complete content creation including graphics, videos, and copywriting. Alternatively, we can work with your existing content or brand assets. Many clients prefer a hybrid approach where we create campaign content while they provide raw materials like photos and company updates.

What's a good ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) to expect?

ROAS varies significantly by industry, product price, and campaign objectives. E-commerce typically targets 3-5x ROAS. Lead generation businesses measure cost per lead and lifetime value. We'll establish benchmarks for your industry and optimise toward those targets. The key is ensuring ad spend generates profitable returns.

How do you measure success?

We track metrics aligned with your business goals - not vanity metrics like likes. For e-commerce: ROAS, conversion rate, cost per acquisition. For lead gen: cost per lead, lead quality, MQLs. For awareness: reach, brand lift, engagement rate. You'll get monthly reports with clear insights and recommendations.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely. Brand consistency is crucial for recognition and trust. We work within your existing guidelines for colors, fonts, voice, and imagery. If you don't have formal guidelines, we can help develop a social media style guide that ensures consistent brand presentation across all platforms.

What's included in your management services?

Our social media management includes strategy, content creation, scheduling, community management, monitoring, and reporting. PPC management includes campaign setup, ongoing optimisation, A/B testing, audience management, creative refreshes, and detailed reporting. All services include regular strategy calls with your dedicated team.

How much do your social media services cost?

Pricing depends on scope - number of platforms, posting frequency, and whether you need paid campaign management. Social media management typically starts from $1,500/month. PPC management starts from $1,000/month plus ad spend. We'll provide a custom proposal based on your specific needs and goals.
