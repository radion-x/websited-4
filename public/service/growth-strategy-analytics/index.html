Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need strategy consulting if I'm already doing marketing?

Especially if you're already investing in marketing. Without proper strategy and measurement, you could be wasting significant budget on underperforming channels. Our clients typically find 20-40% of their spend is underperforming and can be reallocated for better results.

How often should we meet for strategy sessions?

Most clients benefit from monthly check-ins with quarterly deep-dive strategy sessions. The rhythm depends on your pace of change, budget, and complexity. Some fast-moving businesses prefer weekly calls, while others are fine with monthly reviews.

What data do you need from me?

At minimum: Google Analytics access, Google Ads/Meta Ads access (if applicable), CRM access, and any historical sales data. The more data sources we can connect, the more complete picture we can build. We handle all integrations and never share your data.

Can you work with my existing marketing team or agency?

Absolutely. We often work alongside in-house teams and other agencies as the strategy and analytics layer. We provide the data-driven insights and strategic direction, while your team or agency handles execution. Clear communication ensures everyone's aligned.

What's the difference between GA4 and Universal Analytics?

GA4 is Google's latest analytics platform, replacing Universal Analytics (which was sunset in July 2023). GA4 uses an event-based model, offers better cross-device tracking, and has built-in machine learning. If you haven't migrated, you're likely missing critical data.

How much does strategy consulting cost?

Pricing varies based on scope and complexity. One-time audits start around $2,000. Ongoing consulting retainers typically range from $2,000-$5,000/month depending on the depth of involvement. The ROI from better decisions typically far exceeds the investment.

Will you help implement recommendations or just advise?

Both. We can be purely advisory if you have a team to execute, or we can handle implementation ourselves. For analytics setup, dashboard building, and testing, we typically handle the technical work. For marketing tactics, we can execute or guide your team.
