Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Websited Digital Marketing

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm AEST). For urgent matters, call us directly at (07) 5405 2521 for immediate assistance.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our initial consultation is completely free with no obligation. We'll discuss your needs, answer your questions, and provide initial recommendations at no cost. There's no pressure to commit.

What should I prepare for the consultation?

Have a general idea of your business goals, current challenges, and budget range. If you have existing marketing materials, website analytics, or competitor examples, those are helpful but not required. We'll guide you through the rest.

Do you work with businesses outside Sydney?

Yes! While we're based in Sydney, we work with clients across all of Australia and internationally. All our services can be delivered remotely via video calls, screen sharing, and digital collaboration tools like Slack and Asana.

What's your minimum project size?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises. Our minimum project engagement starts at $2,000, and we offer scalable solutions to match your budget and growth stage.

Can I speak to references?

Absolutely! We're happy to provide references from clients in similar industries. Just ask during your consultation and we'll connect you with relevant testimonials and case studies that demonstrate our results.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including monthly payment plans for larger projects. We can also structure retainer agreements for ongoing services. We'll discuss payment terms that work for your business during the consultation.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

We need to understand your business goals, target audience, current digital presence, and budget range. The more details you provide about your project scope, timeline, and desired outcomes, the more accurate our quote will be.
