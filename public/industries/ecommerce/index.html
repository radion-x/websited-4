Digital Marketing for E-commerce Success

The e-commerce landscape is more competitive than ever. With rising customer acquisition costs, marketplace competition, and increasingly savvy shoppers, online retailers need sophisticated marketing strategies to stand out and grow profitably.

At Websited, we specialize in e-commerce marketing that drives measurable results. From conversion rate optimisation and Google Shopping campaigns to email automation and customer retention programs, we help online stores increase revenue while improving return on ad spend.

Challenges E-commerce Businesses Face

We understand the unique obstacles that online retailers encounter when trying to scale profitably in a competitive marketplace.

1
Cart Abandonment The average cart abandonment rate is over 70%. Customers add items to their cart but leave without purchasing due to unexpected costs, complicated checkouts, or simple distractions. Each abandoned cart represents lost revenue that's often recoverable with the right strategies.
2
Rising Customer Acquisition Costs Facebook CPMs have increased dramatically, Google Shopping is more competitive, and getting new customers costs more every year. Without proper optimisation and diversification, ad costs eat into margins making growth unsustainable.
3
Competition from Marketplaces Amazon, eBay, and other marketplaces dominate search results and consumer attention. Building your own brand and driving direct traffic requires strategic differentiation and multi-channel presence to compete effectively.
4
Inventory Visibility & Product Discovery Getting your products found by the right shoppers at the right time is increasingly difficult. Poor product page SEO, unoptimized feeds, and weak content mean products get lost in a sea of competitors.
5
Customer Retention & Lifetime Value Many e-commerce businesses focus solely on acquisition while neglecting existing customers. Without retention strategies, you're constantly on the hamster wheel of expensive new customer acquisition instead of building sustainable, profitable relationships.
6
Analytics & Attribution Complexity With iOS privacy changes and complex customer journeys, tracking what's actually driving sales is harder than ever. Without proper attribution, you can't optimise spend or understand which channels deliver the best returns.

How Websited Helps E-commerce Businesses Grow

Our comprehensive e-commerce marketing solutions address every challenge you face, from acquisition to retention and everything in between.

CRO
Conversion Rate Optimisation

Turn more visitors into buyers with data-driven CRO. We analyze user behavior, run A/B tests, optimise product pages, simplify checkout flows, and remove friction points. A 1% conversion rate improvement can mean thousands in additional revenue. We focus on high-impact optimizations that move the needle on your bottom line.

EML

Build automated email flows that generate revenue on autopilot. Welcome sequences, post-purchase flows, win-back campaigns, and promotional series that drive repeat purchases. Recover lost sales with sophisticated multi-channel cart abandonment sequences. Timed email reminders, SMS nudges, retargeting ads, and exit-intent popups work together to bring shoppers back. Our clients typically recover 15-25% of abandoned carts, representing significant recovered revenue.

Increase customer lifetime value with strategic retention marketing. Loyalty programs, VIP segments, replenishment reminders, cross-sell sequences, and reactivation campaigns. It costs 5x more to acquire a new customer than retain an existing one - we help you maximize the value of every customer relationship.

SEO
SEO for Product Pages

Get your products found organically with e-commerce SEO. Optimised product titles, descriptions, and metadata. Category page optimisation, internal linking strategies, and technical SEO for faster load times. Build sustainable organic traffic that reduces dependence on paid advertising over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our e-commerce marketing services.

Do you integrate with my e-commerce platform?

Yes, we work with all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, Squarespace, and custom-built stores. Our team has extensive experience integrating marketing tools, analytics, and automation systems with these platforms. We can also help migrate your store to a new platform if needed.

How do you manage ad spend for my online store?

We use a data-driven approach to ad spend management. We start with thorough market research and competitor analysis, then allocate budget based on your goals and expected ROAS. We continuously monitor performance, shifting budget to top-performing campaigns and products. You'll receive detailed reports showing exactly where your money goes and what returns it generates.

Can you help with shipping and returns messaging?

Absolutely. Clear shipping and returns policies are crucial for conversion optimisation. We help craft compelling shipping messaging (free shipping thresholds, delivery timeframes), optimise your returns policy for trust while protecting your margins, and ensure this information is prominently displayed where it reduces purchase friction.

What's the difference between B2B and B2C e-commerce marketing?

B2B e-commerce typically involves longer sales cycles, multiple decision makers, and focuses on relationship building, account-based marketing, and detailed product specifications. B2C focuses on emotional triggers, impulse purchases, social proof, and volume. We tailor our strategies accordingly, whether you're selling to businesses, consumers, or both.

Do you help with international selling?

Yes, we help e-commerce businesses expand internationally. This includes international SEO (hreflang tags, country-specific content), multi-currency setup, localised advertising campaigns, international shipping messaging, and market-specific strategies. We've helped Australian businesses successfully sell to the US, UK, Europe, and Asia.

How do you reduce cart abandonment?

We implement multi-touch abandoned cart recovery including email sequences, SMS reminders, and retargeting ads. We also optimise the checkout process to reduce abandonment in the first place - simplifying forms, adding trust signals, offering multiple payment options, and addressing common objections. Our clients typically recover 15-25% of abandoned carts.

What ROAS should I expect from e-commerce advertising?

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) varies by industry, margins, and competition. Generally, we target a minimum 4x ROAS for sustainable growth - meaning $4 revenue for every $1 in ad spend. Many of our clients achieve 6-10x ROAS once campaigns are optimised. We'll set realistic targets based on your specific margins and market conditions.

How long until I see results from e-commerce marketing?

Paid advertising (Google Shopping, Meta Ads) can generate sales within the first week. Conversion rate optimisation typically shows measurable improvements within 30-60 days. Email marketing automation starts generating revenue immediately once flows are set up. SEO takes 3-6 months to show significant organic traffic growth. We provide regular reports so you can track progress from day one.
