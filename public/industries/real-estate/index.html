Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions real estate professionals ask about our marketing services.

Do you work with individual agents or agencies?

We work with both individual real estate agents and full agencies. For individual agents, we focus on personal branding and lead generation. For agencies, we offer scalable solutions that can manage multiple agents, offices, and listing portfolios while maintaining brand consistency. Whether you're a solo agent or running a team of 50, we have solutions that fit.

Can you integrate with my MLS?

Yes, we have experience integrating with major Australian property listing platforms and MLS systems. We can connect your website with realestate.com.au, Domain, and other major portals to automatically sync listings, ensuring your website always displays current inventory. We also work with popular CRMs like Rex, Agentbox, and PropertyMe.

How do you generate seller leads?

We use a multi-channel approach including Google Ads targeting "sell my house" keywords, Facebook lead ads for homeowners, property valuation landing pages, local SEO for "real estate agent near me" searches, and retargeting campaigns. We also create content marketing strategies around market reports and suburb guides that attract homeowners thinking about selling.

What social platforms work best for real estate?

Facebook and Instagram are the top performers for real estate marketing. Facebook is excellent for targeting homeowners and generating leads through its advanced demographic targeting. Instagram works well for showcasing properties through photos, videos, and Stories. LinkedIn is valuable for commercial real estate and building professional referral networks. We'll recommend the right mix based on your target market.

How do you help with personal branding?

We develop comprehensive personal branding strategies including professional photography, video content, social media presence optimisation, thought leadership content, email signature design, and consistent visual branding across all touchpoints. We help position you as the go-to expert in your local market through suburb guides, market updates, and community involvement.

Can you automate my follow-up process?

Absolutely! We implement CRM automation that handles lead nurturing through email drip campaigns, SMS follow-ups, task reminders, and lead scoring. Your leads are automatically segmented and nurtured based on their behavior, so no opportunity falls through the cracks. Typical agents see their follow-up rate jump from 20% to 85% with automation.

What's the typical cost per lead?

Cost per lead varies by market competition and lead type. Buyer leads typically range from $15-50, while seller leads (higher value) range from $50-150. We optimise campaigns continuously to reduce costs while improving lead quality. ROI is what matters - one closed deal can return 10-50x your marketing investment. We track cost per closing, not just cost per lead.

How do you handle multiple office locations?

We create location-specific landing pages optimised for local SEO, manage separate Google Business Profiles for each office, run geo-targeted campaigns, and set up lead routing to ensure inquiries go to the correct office. We maintain brand consistency while allowing each location to target its specific market and showcase local expertise.
