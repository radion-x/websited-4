Digital Marketing for Hospitality & Tourism

The hospitality industry faces unique digital challenges. Online travel agencies take hefty commissions, review platforms control your reputation, and competition for visibility is fierce. Your business needs a strategic digital presence that drives direct bookings, builds customer loyalty, and maximizes revenue per guest.

At Websited, we specialize in hospitality marketing that puts you back in control. From booking-optimised websites to reputation management and social media that makes mouths water, we help hotels, restaurants, tour operators, and event venues fill every room and every seat profitably.

Challenges Hospitality Businesses Face

We understand the unique obstacles that hotels, restaurants, and tourism operators encounter when trying to grow their business in a competitive hospitality market.

1
OTA Dependency Booking.com, Expedia, and other OTAs take 15-25% commission on every booking. Over-reliance on these platforms erodes your margins and puts your business at the mercy of algorithm changes and competitor bidding wars.
2
Seasonal Demand Fluctuations Peak seasons are too short, and low seasons see empty rooms and tables. Without strategic marketing, you're scrambling during slow periods while missing opportunities to maximize revenue during busy times.
3
Reputation Management One negative TripAdvisor review can cost you thousands in lost bookings. Managing your reputation across Google, TripAdvisor, Yelp, Facebook, and industry-specific platforms requires constant vigilance and the right systems.
4
Intense Local Competition Every suburb has multiple restaurants, hotels, and tour operators competing for the same customers. Standing out in local search results and capturing nearby searchers requires strategic local SEO and paid advertising.
5
Direct Booking Optimisation Even when guests want to book direct, clunky websites, slow loading times, and poor mobile experiences drive them back to OTAs. Converting website visitors into direct bookings requires optimised user experience and compelling incentives.
6
Building Guest Loyalty First-time guests rarely return without nurturing. Without email marketing, loyalty programs, and ongoing engagement, you're constantly spending to acquire new customers instead of maximizing lifetime value of existing guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our hospitality marketing services.

How can I reduce my OTA commission fees from Booking.com and Expedia?

We help reduce OTA dependency through direct booking optimisation. This includes creating a high-converting booking website with rate parity or better pricing, implementing a strong SEO strategy to capture direct searches, building email marketing campaigns for repeat guests, and creating loyalty programs that incentivize direct bookings. Most clients see a 25-40% increase in direct bookings within 6 months, significantly reducing commission costs.

How do you handle seasonal demand fluctuations in hospitality marketing?

We develop comprehensive seasonal marketing strategies that include ramping up advertising before peak seasons to capture early bookings, creating off-season promotions and packages to maintain occupancy, adjusting keyword targeting based on seasonal search trends, email campaigns to past guests promoting shoulder season deals, and content marketing around seasonal events and attractions. We also use data analytics to predict demand patterns and adjust budgets accordingly.

How do you manage reviews across multiple platforms like TripAdvisor, Google, and Yelp?

We implement a centralized reputation management system that monitors all major review platforms including TripAdvisor, Google, Yelp, Facebook, and industry-specific sites. Our system includes real-time alerts for new reviews, sentiment analysis to identify trends, professional response templates customized for your brand voice, and automated review request systems that encourage satisfied guests to share their experiences. We also provide monthly reports analyzing review performance and areas for improvement.

Can you help optimise our presence on food delivery apps like Uber Eats and DoorDash?

Yes, we help restaurants maximize their delivery app performance through menu optimisation, professional food photography, strategic pricing, keyword-rich descriptions, and promotional campaign management. We also help you balance delivery app orders with direct orders through your website to reduce commission costs. Our strategies typically improve delivery app visibility and order volume by 30-50% while building direct ordering channels.

What's the best way to market a hotel during low season?

Low season marketing requires a multi-channel approach: targeting domestic travelers and staycationers through local SEO and Google Ads, creating attractive packages bundling accommodation with experiences, partnering with local businesses for cross-promotion, email campaigns to past guests with exclusive off-season rates, content marketing around indoor activities and seasonal attractions, and targeting corporate travelers for business stays. We also recommend adjusting your messaging to emphasize value and unique low-season experiences.

How important is social media for restaurants?

Social media is essential for restaurant marketing. Instagram and TikTok drive significant discovery for dining venues, with 75% of diners checking social media before choosing a restaurant. We create mouth-watering visual content, user-generated content campaigns, influencer partnerships, and targeted advertising that showcases your food, ambiance, and unique experiences. Social media also provides excellent ROI for promoting events, seasonal menus, and special offers.

How can I compete with larger hotel chains in my area?

Independent hotels and smaller properties can compete effectively by emphasizing unique selling points that chains cannot offer: authentic local experiences, personalized service, character and charm, local partnerships, and community connections. We help you tell your unique story through content marketing, target niche audiences actively seeking boutique experiences, optimise for long-tail keywords chains ignore, and build a strong local presence through community engagement and local SEO. Many travelers specifically prefer independent properties.

What results can I expect from hospitality digital marketing?

Results vary by business type and competition, but typical outcomes include: 30-50% increase in direct bookings within 6 months, 40-60% improvement in online visibility and search rankings, significant increase in positive reviews (average rating improvement of 0.5+ stars), 20-35% reduction in OTA dependency, improved table turnover for restaurants, and measurable ROI on advertising spend. We provide detailed monthly reporting so you can track progress across all metrics.
